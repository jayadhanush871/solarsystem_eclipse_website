Solar System & Eclipses

The Solar System

The Solar System contains the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets. The Sun provides heat and energy to all planets and controls their motion using gravity.

Eclipses

Eclipses occur when a celestial body moves into the shadow of another. Solar eclipses occur during New Moon, while lunar eclipses occur during Full Moon.
